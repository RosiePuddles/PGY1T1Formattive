A comprehensive report into global carbon dioxide emissions

This report analyses our global carbon dioxide emissions; from out current emissions, trends in emissions, legislation that could alter this trend, and the impacts it will have on our world in the future

Authors Amanda Hightower, Linda Anthony, Richard M. Camire, Kevin Isom, Kellie A. Ross
Publishing date 19th August 2020
