What our reports are

Our reports are independently funded reports written by individuals with specialist knowledge in the area they're writing about. They are comprehensive and often take many months of research to complete, with a vetting process before we publish anything.

Conflicts of interest

We fund out research through anonymous donations, and ensure that no individual working on a report has any possible conflicts of interest with the contents or subject of the report. In the event of an individual having been found to have a conflict of interest after a report has been published, the report will be retracted and put up for review by an independent review panel to assess any sections of the report that may have been affected by this conflict of interest. If none can be found the report is un-retracted.

Who can use our reports?

All of our reports are free to use and ready by anyone. Anyone is more than welcome to reference any of our work or quote anything provided the report is referenced and credited appropriately.
