The effects of nitrogen poisoning on dense populations through the excessive use of industrial fertilisers

This report looks at the short-, medium-, and long-term effects of continuous nitrogen poisoning on large populations in relation to the use of industrial fertilisers. It also looks at how we can mitigate these effects, with various possible solutions.

Authors Judy Dunbar, Lukáš Tuček, Pataky Melinda
Publishing date 35th June 2022
